Toronto · Non-profit Dental Outreach

Providing Dental Care for Canadians Without Coverage

Our mission is to provide affordable dental care to Torontonians with nowhere else to turn.

We are a non-profit organization that uses existing community dental clinics across the city to provide affordable urgent dental treatment to low-income adults and others facing barriers to care. Services are generously provided by volunteer dentists and hygienists.
